April Member

Yoon Chae-kyung

Yoon Chae-kyung (born July 7, 1996), is a South Korean singer. Yoon debuted as a member of Puretty under DSP in Japan during 2012 and the group later disbanded in January 2014. Following the disbandment, Yoon stayed with the label and appeared as a contestant in the 2014 Kara's reality show for searching new member Kara Project. She also appeared in the reality show Produce 101 and later in the mockumentary series The God of Music 2 which she became a member of project group C.I.V.A and also joined fan-made group of Produce 101, I.B.I both respectively in 2016. Yoon later were added as a member of DSP girl group April in 2017.
